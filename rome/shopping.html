Best Places To Shop In Rome


Also: See the best restuarants in Rome


1. Via Dei Condotti


This is the one stop shopping location for the best shopping experience. This popular street is well known for the designer shopping outlets in the vicinity, such as Dolce & Gabbana, Dior and Gucci. If you are ever looking to buy some designer clothes, especially designer bags, then this is the place for you.


2. La Rinascente


As in the name, La Rinascente, is home to one of the multi-brand luxury store which houses everything from branded cosmetics to home decor.


3. Via Cola Di Rienzo


Luxury is not everything even if it is in the fashion capital of the world. For those who do not plan on going luxury shopping but still want a taste of fashion in Roma, then Via Cola Di Rienzo is the place for you. It is a popular street amongst the locals which offers a mix of some Italian and international brands.


4. Via del Governo Vecchio


A shopping spree in Roma would not be complete if you do not take a peak at the jewellery and antiquites. While they may not be the shiniest and overly spectecular, it is one place that you should check out, especially the boutiques and jewellery stores.


5. Spanish Steps


If you think that your trip to Rome simply won’t be complete without splurging on a real Fendi purse, a bauble of Gucci or a wallet from Prada, then look no further than the streets fanning out from Piazza di Spagna (most famously known as the Spanish Steps). The amount of credit card debt that gets run up on Via Condotti, Via Borgognona, Via del Babuino and Via Frattina, is a little woorying since all the big players are there.


6. Via Margutta


Next on the list is a place known not for the clothing retailers it has, but for art galleries. This is the place to be for all those who appreciate art, and the best part of it all is that is affordable shopping.


7. Castel Romano


Castel Romano, which might as well be a go to place for a lot of people, is a shopping outlet which offers famous designer brands like Michael Kors, Roberto Cavalli, Nike, and more at 30 to 70% off throughout the year. What more could you ask for!


8. Monti


Last but not least, we have Monti. While it might not have the best retail designer outlets, it certainly boasts its fair share of designer clothes from upcoming designers. The streets of Monti are also lined up with resale shops, bijou jewellers and the studio-storefronts
