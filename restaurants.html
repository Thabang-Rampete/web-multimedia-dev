Best Places To Grab a Bite in Rome


Choosing a place to eat at in Rome really depends on how much you have. Folks looking to grab a bite are spoiled for choice becuase of the various places they can eat at such as Trattorias and Pizzerias. Amyway here are the best resuarants to dine at in Rome.


1. Epiro

This is a very popular spot that people like to eat at. It serves modern dishes, natural wines and foraged ingredients. Epiro was among one of the first contemporary local resaurants to serve truly a satisfying entrée that combined flavors, colors and textures in a novel way.


2. Fratelli Mori

Fratelli Mori is a restaurant named after two brother, Alessandro and Francesco Mori, who shared a passion for cooking.Unlike other famous restuarants, Fratelli Mori does not sell over the top food but does well with some of the local Italian cuisine such as meatballs and woodfire pizzas.


3. Bistrot64

Hailed as one of the most affordable restaurant, Bistrot64 is a place worth visiting because of its incredible combination of Italian and Japanese cuisine. Not only does the food taste exquisite, but the presentation is on point as well.


4. Alcrove

Altrove is a restaurant that aims to achive social awarness through food. It offers a variety of some of the finest International cuisines and works with multicultural staff and marginalized young people to encorage them to hone their skills as chefs.


5. Fernanda

Fernanda is a restaurant that plays a whole different ball game when it comes to the culinary delights it serves. This is all thanks to the chefs whom studied together, on fermented vegetables. While this might not sound the best thing, it is simply one of best part about the dishes. The desserts are also a thing of beauty.
